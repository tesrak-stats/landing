SPX ATR Roadmap

A visual probability engine based on the Saty Mahajan level system, powered by 10 years of SPX 10-minute candles (2014–2024).

What does it show?

  • 📈 Odds of reaching key ATR levels after a trigger
  • 🕒 Hour-by-hour breakdown of goal completions
  • 🔁 Includes both continuation and retracement probabilities
  • 🧠 Based on over 2,700 trading days of SPX price data
